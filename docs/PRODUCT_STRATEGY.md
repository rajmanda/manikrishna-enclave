# PRODUCT_STRATEGY.md

Last updated: 2026-07-03 · Draft — owner input welcome, especially pricing/GTM.

## Vision

Every apartment community runs on one transparent platform instead of
WhatsApp groups, Excel sheets, paper invoices and lost receipts.

## Mission

Give property managers and owners a single mobile-first place for money,
maintenance, records and decisions — simple enough for non-technical elders.

## Customer personas

| Persona | Reality today | What CommunityHub gives them |
|---|---|---|
| **Vishnu — property manager** | Chases dues on WhatsApp, tracks expenses in Excel, keeps receipts in a folder | Dashboards, invoice tracking, work-order pipeline, vendor records, audit trail |
| **Resident owner** (e.g. Apt 502) | No visibility into where maintenance money goes | Live expense ledger, own invoices/receipts, work-order status, votes |
| **Senior/NRI owner** (e.g. Apt 501) | Depends on phone calls; can't attend meetings | Transparency from anywhere, meeting minutes, polls, statements |
| **Auditor** | Annual shoebox of paper | Read-only access to ledgers and the audit log |

## Market & competitors (India focus)

MyGate/NoBrokerHood (security/visitor-first, HOA features bolted on),
ApnaComplex/ADDA (feature-rich but dated UX, priced for large societies),
WhatsApp+Excel (the real competitor). **Wedge:** small societies (5–50
units) that big platforms overserve and overprice — start free/cheap,
transparency-first, genuinely mobile.

## Monetization (hypotheses, not commitments)

1. Free for the seed community; learn.
2. Per-apartment/month SaaS (₹20–50/apt/mo) once multi-community.
3. Later: payment-collection convenience fees, premium AI features
   (OCR, summaries, forecasting).

## GTM

Prove it at Mani Krishna Enclave → word-of-mouth via Vishnu's network of
managers → self-serve community onboarding (already multi-tenant by design).

## Success metrics

- Seed community: % dues collected on time, % expenses with receipts,
  weekly active owners, work orders resolved via the app.
- Platform: communities onboarded, retention, NPS from property managers.

## Strategic principles

Multi-tenant from day one · API-first (mobile app is Phase 5, not a rewrite) ·
transparency is the product · boring reliable tech.
